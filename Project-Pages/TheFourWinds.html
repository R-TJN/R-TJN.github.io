The Four Winds

Abstract

   my friend Mark and I are both members of our schools UAV(Unmanned Arial Vehicles) club, which is in the schools AIAA(American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics) chapter. The club mainly acts as a place for people who make RC drones/planes their hobby to gather, but it also offers the oportunity for people who wish to build RC drones/planes from scratch funding to do so. Upon learning this, Mark and I decided that we rather like planes, and really like other people paying for them. We approached the club leader Cameron, who is the best ♥, and put forth a proposal to build 4 slow trainer planes out of foamboard which could be lent out to people to learn to fly. Cameron got us the funding and we are currently finnishing up the first prototype plane!
